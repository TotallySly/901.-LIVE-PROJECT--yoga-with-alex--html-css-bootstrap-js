Hej! I'm Alex
After years of struggling with depression, anxiety and being overstressed by work I finally had enough and turned to yoga.
I was hoping that yoga would help me manage my stress and anxiety but what I found was so much more deeper than that. Having a regular asana and meditation practice helped me to find my way home to myself.
Now I would like to invite you to take a step back and slow down. To move gently and mindfully. With compassion, patience and non-judgement towards ourselves we can start healing whatever needs healing inside of us. I am here for you, I will hold your hand, wipe the tears and laugh with you.
